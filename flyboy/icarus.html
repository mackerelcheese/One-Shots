they forget to warn you

 

 

 

about how far the fall is

 

 

how fast you sink

 

 

the way the water

 

 

soaks your feathers
the water is cold

 

your lungs can't find air

 

but don't forget

 

you can still

 

 

turn things around

 

and one day

 

 

 

you will learn

 

 

 

to fly again.
