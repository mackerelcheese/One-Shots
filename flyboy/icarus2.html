no one prepares you

for when the wax melts.

they don't know how far the fall is

or how easy it is to sink midair.

it will hurt.

and when you flew too high

they shook their head

and said it was your fault

for wanting to touch the sunlight.

but they do not understand

the appeal of warmth

and they forgotten

that when you fly too low

the water will soak your feathers

and drag you under.

you will drown.

your lungs will deflate.

and the water is cold.

you can't feel your fingers.

it is easier

to sink

than swim.

but don't you forget

you can still

turn things around.
and one day
you can grow wings
and learn to fly again.
