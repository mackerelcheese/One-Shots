find my friends

by mackerel_cheese

Summary

As far as he knew, none of his friends had any plans today. It would take him forever to hunt for them through the castle.

Unless…

Remus pulls out the blank parchment from his nightstand where they stashed it after a long night of fiddling with the spellwork. The innocuous piece of paper was so much more than what meets the eye - the beginnings of their latest project, a map of the school.

Or: Remus is lonely and tests out their new map to look for his friends.

Or, or: The proof of concept for a prototype interactive Marauders Map workskin I’m making poorly disguised as a Choose Your Own Adventure fluff fic. Click away, my friends.
